Techniques

Flame Knives

advance & attack

Mark up to 3-fatigue. Hold an equal number of flames. Lose 1-flame at the end of each exchange after this one. When you inflict fatigue or conditions on a foe, inflict an additional 1-fatigue for each remaining flame.


Conditions

Hurl Metal*

advance & attack


metal

Rip and bend big pieces of metal in the surrounding area to strike at a foe. Mark 1-fatigue and inflict 2-fatigue or one condition, your

choice, on your target. They also either lose a positive status or suffer

Impaired as they are knocked off-balance.


Refresh

evade & observe


heal

Apply water to reinvigorate and close wounds on a willing target. Mark fatigue to heal an ally in reach who is evading and observing. Clear an appropriate status from them, and clear 3-fatigue or two conditions.


*Rare Technique

APPENDIX C ‌

PLAY

MATERIALS


This appendix includes useful references for playing Avatar Legends: The Roleplaying Game. There are four sheets included here: a basic moves reference, a combat exchange reference, a campaign creation worksheet, and a GM reference. While all of the infor- mation on these pages is in this book, these reference sheets collect the rules in one place to make them easy to use in actual play. For the game as a whole, you need one campaign creation worksheet and one GM reference; for each individual player (including the GM), you need one basic moves reference and one combat exchange reference. There are digi- tal versions of these play materials, including additional NPC tracking sheets, available at magpiegames.com/avatarrpg.
