Background on p.65


“It is important to draw wisdom from many different places.”

Drive


Learn from the past and cultivate the future


How to Play General Iroh

• Be warm, gracious, and regretful. Iroh tries his best to make others feel welcome in his presence and doesn’t let his ego get the better of him. He is dealing with the relatively fresh tragedy of his son’s death, which drove him to abdicate his position as heir to the throne; that sadness still touches him.

• Prize wisdom like a cup of finely brewed tea. Knowledge is the key to greatness and Iroh knows this better than most. He’s an avid listener and has a way of teasing information out of others that they might not have even realized they knew themselves.

• Cultivate greatness. Iroh has the knack for being in the right place at the right time and bringing out the best in people. He notices the spark in people that others might miss and endeavors to guide people along the right path.

Bring Iroh in…

• …when a hero needs bolstering from an unlikely source.

• …when the White Lotus needs something done.

• …when someone threatens to upset the spiritual balance.
