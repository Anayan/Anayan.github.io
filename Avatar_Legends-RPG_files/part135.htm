Kyoshi ‌

“…only justice will bring peace.”

Drive

Balance by any means necessary


Background on p.32


How to Play Kyoshi

• Be direct. The Avatar is fierce and assertive. No matter what her internal feelings may be, Kyoshi makes her voice heard.

• Take risks and seize the moment. Kyoshi is an Avatar of action and, when she needs to, she acts decisively and swiftly. Kyoshi has no qualms about knocking a few heads together if it gets results.

• Prioritize justice. She may not be the best at reading people, but Kyoshi knows right from wrong. She grew up with nothing and saw how people in power abused their position; there is no way that’s happening under her watch.

Bring Kyoshi in…

• …when people in power hurt those they are meant to protect.

• …when a hero doubts what to do and the stakes are high.

• …when a hard decision needs to be made by someone important.
