Roku ‌


Background on p.44


“It was bitter work, but the results were worth it.”

Drive

Maintain the peace and show restraint


How to Play Roku

• Exude competence. Roku is a skilled Avatar. He’s taken the time required to know what his job entails and does it exceptionally well.

• Find the middle ground. Roku’s work as Avatar is largely focused on maintaining peace between the Four Nations. He does everything in his power to negotiate, calm, and guide potentially volatile situations.

• Appreciate novelty and individuality. The Avatar has traveled extensively and knows the world is full of beautiful unique things. He believes people are stronger when their talents are valued and takes the time to listen to what others have to say.

Bring Roku in…

• …when important matters are about to escalate to violence.

• …when the companions have meddled in political affairs beyond their ken.

• …when a critical agreement has been broken.


Techniques

Breath of Wind

advance & attack

Exhale mightily through pursed lips. Mark 1-fatigue to knock down a single target and inflict Stunned on them unless they mark 4-fatigue.


Fire Stream

defend & maneuver

Pour fire upon a target. Mark 1-fatigue to inflict Impaired on them. Mark 3-fatigue to inflict Doomed and Impaired. Mark 5-fatigue to inflict Trapped, Doomed, and Impaired.


Lava Wave*

advance & attack


lava

Raise and push a wave of lava from the earth around you. The wave destroys and sets fire to anything in the environment that it touches. Foes in its path have to fling themselves out of the way, marking

2-fatigue and becoming Impaired, or becoming Stunned and Doomed

as the wave knocks them to the side and catches them aflame.
