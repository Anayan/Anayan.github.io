Kyoshi ‌

“…only justice will bring peace.”

Drive

Balance by any means necessary


Background on p.32


How to Play Kyoshi

• Be direct. The Avatar is fierce and assertive. No matter what her internal feelings may be, Kyoshi makes her voice heard.

• Take risks and seize the moment. Kyoshi is an Avatar of action and, when she needs to, she acts decisively and swiftly. Kyoshi has no qualms about knocking a few heads together if it gets results.

• Prioritize justice. She may not be the best at reading people, but Kyoshi knows right from wrong. She grew up with nothing and saw how people in power abused their position; there is no way that’s happening under her watch.

Bring Kyoshi in…

• …when people in power hurt those they are meant to protect.

• …when a hero doubts what to do and the stakes are high.

• …when a hard decision needs to be made by someone important.


Techniques

Breath of Fire

advance & attack

Breathe fire in a massive gout. Mark 1-fatigue to set alight as much or as little of your surroundings as you choose and try to set aflame any foes within reach. Those foes must either retreat and disengage with you, becoming Impaired, or suffer 2-fatigue and become Doomed as they catch fire.


Dust-Stepping

defend & maneuver

Step up into the air on thin pillars of dust and stone. Advance to a higher position and become Favored and Prepared. Any foe engaged with you can mark 2-fatigue to block this effect.


Earthquake*

advance & attack

Ripple the earth and stone all around you with terrible force. Any struc- tures around you are damaged or destroyed, inflicting up to 4-fatigue (GM’s choice) on anyone inside and Impairing and Trapping them.


*Rare Technique
