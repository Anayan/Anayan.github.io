APPENDIX B ‌

NPCS


This appendix includes several pre-built NPCs split between minor, major, master, groups, and legendary categories. The NPCs here are useful for a GM who quickly needs inspira- tion for an NPC’s mechanical features; any of these NPCs can be adapted to fit several dif- ferent kinds of character. When adapting one of these NPCs, pay attention to the balance principle, the drive, and techniques in particu- lar—make sure those fit the NPC you need in the moment. The legends here are all iconic characters from the Avatarverse stories, each with a rare technique they are uniquely suited to use and teach.
