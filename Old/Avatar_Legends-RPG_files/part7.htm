The Earth Kingdom

The largest of the Four Nations, the Earth Kingdom has been ruled by a monarchy for much of its history. It is known for its sheer vastness and diversity of both geography and peoples. De- spite the history of hardship in the rural areas neglected by the government, the citizens of the Earth Kingdom are known for their strength, resilience, and unyielding nature. When they are knocked down, they rarely stay down for long. They draw their strength from the immortal earth and immovable mountains.
